ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

TOPTRACER RANGE

Our range delivers the same ball-tracking technology that traces the shots of the game's best player on TV.

Toptracer Range technology offers a fun, engaging, tech-driven exprience that appeals to seasoned players, range rivals, friends family members, and even first-time golfers.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Adventure awaits you!

Become a Jurassic explorer as you delve into the land of the dinosaurs! Putt your way through prehistoric landscape, cascading waterfalls and meet some dinsaur friends along the way.

GOLF LESSONS
